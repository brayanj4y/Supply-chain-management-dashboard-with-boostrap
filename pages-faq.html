Dashboard Overview
1. What is the purpose of the dashboard?

The dashboard provides an at-a-glance view of the key metrics and overall performance of the supply chain. It is designed to help users quickly assess operations.

2. How can I customize the dashboard?

The dashboard allows for customization of the displayed widgets to match the specific needs of your business operations. You can modify the sections and KPIs shown.

3. Can I track multiple supply chains from the dashboard?

Yes, the dashboard supports monitoring multiple supply chains by aggregating data from various sources, allowing you to switch between different views seamlessly.

Order Tracking

You can track your orders in real-time using the order tracking section, which provides detailed information on order status, processing stages, and estimated delivery dates.

Shipment Tracking

The shipment tracking feature allows you to monitor the location and status of your shipments, providing live updates on delivery progress.

Yes, the system supports tracking multiple shipments, allowing you to follow the progress of all your deliveries in one consolidated view.
Inventory Overview

The inventory overview section provides insights into your stock levels, helping you track available, reserved, and incoming inventory in real-time.

Yes, you can manage stock levels, create alerts for low inventory, and update inventory counts directly from the inventory overview.
